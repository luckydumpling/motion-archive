Emanuele Colombo's Year in Review animation for LinkedIn translates personal data into a celebratory motion experience. The piece works because the animation logic reflects the content: milestones, connections and moments accumulate with the same rhythm they would in a real year. The motion is not applied to the data, it emerges from it.

Colombo's practice sits at the intersection of UX motion and brand animation, working across digital products, branding and interactive systems. His Vimeo channel documents a consistent approach to colour-driven, system-based motion design.
