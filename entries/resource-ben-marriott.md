Ben Marriott is a Sydney-based motion designer and illustrator whose YouTube channel has become one of the most widely used self-directed learning resources in the field. His tutorials cover After Effects technique, workflow efficiency, and 2D character animation, consistently pitched at intermediate level with a focus on transferable principles rather than one-off effects.

The channel is particularly useful for students who have covered the basics and need structured exposure to professional workflow patterns. Marriott teaches process as much as technique, making his approach relevant beyond the specific software version or project type.
