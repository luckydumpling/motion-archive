Part of Jake In Motion's Effects of After Effects series, this short video explains Posterize Time: an effect that reduces the frame rate of a layer independently from the composition, producing a stuttered, hand-drawn feel.

Understanding frame rate as a design variable rather than a technical constraint opens up a distinct visual register. Small concept, wide implications.
