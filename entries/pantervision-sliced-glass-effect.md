PANTERvision's tutorial on the sliced glass effect shows how to simulate refraction and depth in After Effects using only native tools. The technique layers displacement maps, blending modes and colour correction to produce something that reads as optically convincing without requiring plugins.

The underlying logic, using displacement to simulate how light bends through a material, transfers well beyond this specific effect.
