This piece earns its rhythm by treating each word as a physical object with weight. The letters don't simply appear — they arrive, settle, and leave with intention. The easing curves are tuned so that the motion mirrors the stress of the spoken word, making the typography feel like an extension of the voice rather than decoration layered on top of it.

Notice how the layering builds tension in the chorus: elements enter before the previous ones have fully resolved, creating a controlled sense of overlap that keeps the eye moving without losing the read.
