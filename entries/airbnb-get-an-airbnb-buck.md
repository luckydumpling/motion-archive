Buck's Get an Airbnb campaign makes a product comparison, Airbnb versus hotels, entirely through miniature 3D worlds. The playfulness is strategic: small scale, precise craft, one differentiating feature per spot. The copy barely needs to do anything.

The same visual language runs across multiple spots and contexts, a flexible brand device that stays entertaining without losing its strategic edge.
