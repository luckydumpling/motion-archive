Typography as material. Elastic's titles for The Witcher build the entire sequence from letterforms that behave like physical substances — iron, clouds, fluids, light. The type doesn't animate so much as transform, each material carrying a layer of meaning about the world the show inhabits.

The restraint is what makes it work. Nocomplex camera moves, no environment, no characters. Just letters and what they are made of.
