Sponsored by Adobe for Motion Plus Design, _Abstract Figures_ by Caroline Defrance and Melvin Le Riboter is an explicit homage to Bauhaus and De Stijl — movement built from geometric primitives, strict color relationships, and choreographed timing. The piece illustrates that a deliberate formal constraint is not a limitation but rather a system.

With decisions remaining visible and deliberate, this may serve as a more effective model for composition students than technically sophisticated work.
