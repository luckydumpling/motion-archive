Part of a structured expressions challenge series, this tutorial covers one of the most useful and reusable patterns in After Effects: distributing objects along a path using expressions. The technique introduces key expression concepts — indexing, path point access, and systematic thinking — in a practical, immediately applicable context.

aescripts + aeplugins is a trusted resource in the professional motion design community. A good entry point into expressions for students who already understand keyframing.
