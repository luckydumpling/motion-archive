Movie VFX is a curated YouTube channel collecting VFX breakdowns, making-ofs, showreels and CGI shorts from studios and productions worldwide. It functions as a reference library rather than a tutorial resource — the focus is on finished work and the process behind it, not instruction.

For students of motion design and visual effects, breakdown videos are among the most direct forms of technical education available. Seeing a composited shot disassembled into its constituent passes — plates, CG elements, mattes, colour — makes legible what finished work deliberately conceals. The channel's breadth across film, advertising and games makes it useful across disciplines.
