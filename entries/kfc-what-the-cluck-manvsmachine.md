Directed by ManvsMachine for Mother London, *What The Cluck?!* integrates live action and 2D character animation through a series of sudden, high-impact transformations. The technical challenge — seamless hybrid compositing at the pace of a 10-second TV spot — is handled with precision, but the more instructive quality is the energy management. The piece builds pressure and releases it exactly at the right moment, every time.

The campaign demonstrates ManvsMachine's ability to subordinate technical complexity to timing. The transformations are spectacular, but they land because the rhythm earns them.
