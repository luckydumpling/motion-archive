Founded in 2020 by Jamie Quantrill and Winston Duke, Brands in Motion is an independent platform curating motion design specifically within the context of branding. Where general motion design resources cover the full range of the field, this one maintains a deliberate focus: how motion functions as part of a brand identity, not as decoration applied after the fact.

Particularly relevant for understanding how leading studios approach motion as a systemic design problem rather than a series of individual animations.
