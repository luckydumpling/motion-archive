Andrea Codrington's monograph is the only book-length study of Kyle Cooper's work. Published in 2003 as part of Laurence King's Monographics series, it covers his trajectory from R/Greenberg Associates through the founding of Imaginary Forces, with the Se7en titles (1995) as the historical pivot point. The first twenty pages offer biographical context and critical framing with direct quotes from Cooper, followed by a visual survey of sequences and storyboards.

Reviewers noted the book could have gone deeper technically, but as a primary visual document and career overview it remains the only dedicated reference. For students interested in title design history, it is the starting point.
