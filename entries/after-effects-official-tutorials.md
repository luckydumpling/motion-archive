Adobe's official tutorial library for After Effects — the most direct starting point for learning the software. Organised by skill level and topic, from interface basics through to advanced compositing and expressions.

Unlike a linear curriculum, this is a library — students can follow it sequentially or jump to specific techniques. The beginner section covers compositions, keyframes, text animation, and masking. The full library is available at [adobe.com/learn/after-effects](https://www.adobe.com/learn/after-effects).
