Founded in 2004 as a monthly DVD magazine, Stash is the largest online library dedicated to motion design, animation and VFX. Its Permanent Collection provides streaming access to thousands of curated projects with behind-the-scenes features and interviews, making it one of the most comprehensive archives of professional motion work available.

The distinction between Stash and other resources is its depth of documentation. Projects are not merely linked but contextualised through production detail and process, giving students and practitioners access to the thinking behind finished work rather than the work alone.
