Founded in Barcelona in 2001, OFFF is the largest annual gathering in the field of creativity, art and digital design. Each edition commissions a main title sequence from a leading studio or director, creating a continuous series of significant motion design works that has become a reference in its own right.

Beyond the conference programme, OFFF's institutional role is the legitimisation of motion design as a discipline worthy of sustained cultural attention. Its title sequences, published freely online, function as a publicly accessible canon of contemporary motion work.
