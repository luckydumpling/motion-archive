Hornet's opening film for OFFF Barcelona 2024 assembles a dense, layered world from heterogeneous visual material — 3D environments, character animation, archival textures — held together by rhythm rather than narrative logic. The piece doesn't tell a story so much as establish a register: chaotic, generous, referential, alive.

What makes it instructive is the editorial thinking. Individual shots are formally diverse, yet the whole coheres through pacing and sound. This is the work of a studio that understands motion systems — not just how individual elements move, but how they accumulate into a coherent visual experience.
