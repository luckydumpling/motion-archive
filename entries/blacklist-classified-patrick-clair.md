Directed by Patrick Clair at Antibody for NBC Universal, the Blacklist "Classified" trailer uses redacted document aesthetics as its primary visual system. Classified stamps, file numbers, redacted text, and surveillance imagery are assembled into a layered composite that establishes character and threat simultaneously. The typography does not accompany the content, it constitutes it.

The piece reads as a declassified dossier in motion, a formal decision that is both thematically appropriate and visually distinctive.
