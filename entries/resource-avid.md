The Audiovisual Identity Database is a collaborative wiki documenting motion logos, idents and bumpers worldwide. Founded in 2007 and rebranded as AVID in 2022, it holds close to 30,000 articles covering broadcast and production identities across regions and eras. Where TVARK focuses on British broadcast history, AVID has international scope.

Each entry documents visual and audio components with technical description, contextual notes and availability information. Useful for comparative research across broadcast traditions.
