Golden Wolf's campaign film for Coca-Cola Frozen is a showcase of the studio's signature approach: high-energy 2D character animation driven by rhythm and colour saturation. The piece moves at the speed of its subject — frozen, refreshing, immediate — with transitions and holds calibrated to the beat rather than to narrative necessity.

The colour work is particularly considered. Rather than illustrating the product's coolness, the palette enacts it. This kind of alignment between subject and formal decision is what separates craft from decoration.
