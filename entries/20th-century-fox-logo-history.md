This third-party compilation documents over a century of the 20th Century Fox logo in motion, from its origins in 1914 through to the 2020 rebrand as 20th Century Studios. As a single continuous document, it makes visible what is normally experienced only in fragments: the iterative refinement of a single mark across changing technologies, aesthetic conventions, and corporate contexts.

The Fox fanfare and searchlight configuration establish a visual and sonic identity so durable that each revision is measured against it rather than departing from it. For students of motion identity, the compilation is instructive precisely because it shows iteration operating at the scale of decades — the same underlying logic, renegotiated with each era's tools and expectations.
