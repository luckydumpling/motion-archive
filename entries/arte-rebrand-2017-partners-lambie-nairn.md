The 2017 rebrand of ARTE by The Partners and Lambie-Nairn is built around a single governing idea: the channel as a cultural magnet, pulling European culture toward its logo. Every ident in the system enacts this logic differently — material, geography, texture all vary, but the directional pull of the mark remains constant. The concept is not imposed on the motion; it is the motion.

What makes the system instructive is how constraint produces range. The logo rotation and the magnetic pull give every execution a shared visual logic while allowing enormous variation in content and atmosphere. Sound design by Echoic is integral — the identity would not hold without it.
